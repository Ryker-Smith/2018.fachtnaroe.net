1 Small Business Partner Program

1.1 This is a program that has been designed with Small Business Resellers in mind. It delivers sales tools, focused communication, marketing programs, sales and technical training resources, plus support that the Small Business Reseller may need to use to educate the small businesses customer in the benefits of networking.

For more information or to register, please visit the Small Business Web site at http://www.3com.com/smallbusiness which contains links to your local Small Business Web page, (where one exists).

Alternatively, contact your local 3Com Sales Office.
