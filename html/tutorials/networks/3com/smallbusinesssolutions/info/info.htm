1 Warranty and support

1.1 All OfficeConnect hubs, switches, LAN modems and firewalls are covered by a Lifetime Limited Warranty. This means that while the unit is owned by its original purchaser, 3Com commits to repair or replace any defective units or refund the purchase price. (The term "Limited" is used to exclude cases where the OfficeConnect product is damaged in any way by the user.) In addition, the OfficeConnect LAN modems come with a five year Advanced Hardware Replacement pledge. This means that if your hardware needs to be replaced, 3Com will ship you a replacement unit before you return the faulty unit. All OfficeConnect products qualify for free telephone technical support for the first 90 days, provided that the user has registered their product.

The HP JetDirect 170X OfficeConnect Print Server comes with a one year warranty.

The HP JetDirect 300X OfficeConnect Print Server comes with a three year warranty.

Those two products are sold and supported by Hewlett-Packard.

You can now register your OfficeConnect product online by going to http://support.3com.com/warrantyregistration/register.pl

1.2 3Com Support

This OfficeConnect product comes with telephone technical support.

The telephone technical support is available from 3Com from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., local time, Monday through Friday, excluding local holidays. Telephone technical support is limited to the 3Com product designated above and may include assistance with installation, product specific configuration, and identification of equipment problems. Please refer to the Technical Support appendix in the User Guide for telephone numbers.

Response to requests for telephone technical support will be in the form of a return call from a 3Com representative by close of business the following business day.

To qualify for this telephone technical support, Customer must register on the 3Com Web site at http://support.3Com.com/index.htm, and provide the date of purchase, product number, and serial number. 3Com reserves the right to modify or cancel this offering at any time, without advance notice. This offering is not available where prohibited or restricted by law.

Immediate access to electronic product support is available on the 3Com Web site using the Knowledgebase Web service located at http://knowledgebase.3com.com. For further assistance with questions about installing, configuring and troubleshooting your new 3Com product, this interactive troubleshooting tool is accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and is a definitive source of the most current 3Com product information. The 3Com knowledgebase contains thousands of proven solutions to immediately provide customers the answers they are looking for. With over 200,000 registered users, this powerful tool is a popular and effective link to 3Com's collective engineering expertise.
