Ports 1–4 LEDs
The Ethernet Port Status LEDs indicate the status of the Ethernet ports on the unit:
- On indicates the unit detects the Ethernet link integrity signal from an attached computer and operation is normal.
- Flashes when the computer attached to that port is transmitting data to the LAN.
- Off indicates the unit does not detect the Ethernet link integrity signal. This may indicate an improperly connected cable, or the cable maybe the wrong polarity. You must use an Ethernet cable with the 56K LAN Modem.
