Alert LED
Indicates possible problems with the LAN Modem. It may light for one of the following reasons:
- During the power-on self-test diagnostic or after pressing the reset button. Once the unit has passed the diagnostic test and is working properly the ALERT LED will no longer illuminate.
- Flashes (four times per second) if one or more of the diagnostics have failed or after the unit is placed in firmware download mode and is awaiting a firmware upgrade. Refer to the User Manual for further information.
- Flashes (once per second) to indicate pending voice mail messages.
