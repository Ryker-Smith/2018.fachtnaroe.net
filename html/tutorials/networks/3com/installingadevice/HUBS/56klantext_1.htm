Alert LED
Indicates possible problems with the LAN Modem. It may light for one of the following reasons:
- During the power-on self-test diagnostic or after pressing the reset button. Once the unit has passed the diagnostic test and is working properly the ALERT LED will no longer illuminate.
- A fast flash (five blinks per second) indicates that the DHCP server has issued all of the available IP addresses, and is unable to fulfill a new request. This may happen if you have twenty-five computers attached to the LAN Modem, and you replace one with another computer.
