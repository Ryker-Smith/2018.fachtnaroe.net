Port Status LEDs
green/yellow
Indicates the status of each port.
green – The link between the port and the next piece of network equipment is OK.
yellow – The port has partitioned due to a fault on that segment.
Off – Nothing is connected to the port or the link has failed.The 10BASE-2 port LED (Hub 8C only) can only be yellow or off. It is yellow if the port has partitioned.
