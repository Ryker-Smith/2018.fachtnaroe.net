Cannot "Ping" a Device:

There are three main reasons why the PING (Packet Internet Groper) request may fail to locate a PC or laptop on your network:

  • The IP address is wrong.
  • There is a problem with the IP configuration.
  • There is a fault on your network somewhere between the device doing the "pinging" and the PC or laptop being "pinged".

To check the IP address:

Repeat the instructions in Using your network, Checking IP, and check that you enter the correct IP address for the laptop or PC you are trying to ping.
If the ping fails again, check the IP configuration and network connections.

To check IP configuration:

  1. On the PC or laptop you are tying to ping, repeat the instructions given in the Setting up IP section.
    Make sure that you write down the IP address.

  2. On another PC or laptop, repeat the instructions given in the Checking IP section.

  3. To check your basic network connections follow the instruction in Troubleshooting Flow Diagrams.
