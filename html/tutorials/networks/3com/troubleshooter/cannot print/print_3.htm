You Can Print, but the Print Quality is Poor:

If blank paper comes out of the printer or the text/graphics are faded, you may need to install or change the toner or ink cartridge. Refer to the printer's user guide for instructions.

If the printer prints out pages and pages of unexpected numbers, you are printing Postscript. Check that you are using the right driver for your printer.

If the image looks mis-aligned or there are fine white lines throughout the page, you may need to calibrate your printer. Refer to the printer's user guide for instructions.

If your printer allows you to print in Draft or Economy mode, text and graphics may look faded or have a lower resolution than normal. Refer to the printer's user guide for more information.
