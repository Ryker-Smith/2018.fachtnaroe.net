The Configuration Screens are Not Shown when a Plug-and-Play NIC is Installed:

There may be several reasons for this. For example, your PC may not support Plug-and-Play.

Most of these problems can be resolved by configuring the NIC manually, as described in Configuring the NIC.

If you have a laptop refer to Trouble-shooting section "The Configuration Screens are Not Shown when a PCMCIA Card is Installed".

If this does not solve the problem, the NIC may be faulty.

Refer to your supplier for more details on warranty and service.
