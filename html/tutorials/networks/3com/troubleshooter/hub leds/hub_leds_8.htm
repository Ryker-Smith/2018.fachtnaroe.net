An LED Indicates a 10Mbps Connection, Not 100Mbps as Expected:

Each port on an OfficeConnect Dual Speed Hub operates at 10Mbps or 100Mbps, depending on the speed of the connected device. If a device capable of operating at 100Mbps is connected, yet the Dual Speed Hub port indicates a 10Mbps connection:

  • Check that Category 5 cable is used for the connection.

  • The NIC or PCMCIA card of the connected device may be faulty or incorrectly configured. Refer to the NIC or PCMCIA card user guide for more information.
