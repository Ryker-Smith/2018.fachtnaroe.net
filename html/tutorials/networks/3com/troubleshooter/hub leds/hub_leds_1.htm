All LEDs are OFF:

There may be a problem with the power supply or the OfficeConnect device may be faulty. Make sure that:

  1. A 3Com approved and working power cable is securely connected to the OfficeConnect device. Refer to your supplier for details on how to purchase the correct power cable.

  2. The power cable is also connected to a working main power supply socket.
    Tip: You can check that a socket is working by plugging a device you know that works into the socket, for example, a lamp.

  3. If the main power supply socket has a switch, make sure that it is switched to the ON position.
    If the Power LED does not come on, the device may be faulty. Disconnect the device from the power supply, and refer to your supplier for details on warranty and service.
