 The Daily Assembler - A Journey into the Darkness
Robert E. Teto

You've coded in BASIC. You write windows programs in VB with only one hand. You laugh at the pitiful excuses for human life who use Java and call themselves "programmers." You consider yourself a geek because you eat C for breakfast. Well, there is an even darker side to this world of logic my friend. Enter Assembly Language. Welcome to hell ladies, the only easy day was yesterday.

Join me in a journey into the netherworld of computer programming. I will take you step-by-step through a cornucopia of abstract dimensions and twisted logic. Learn instructions that bridge the gap between man and machine as I make journal entries describing my findings. It could be named Teto's 9 Stages of Assembly. Prepare yourself for a journey into darkness.
