    Title: Practical C++ Programming
    Author: Steve Oualline
    Publisher: O'Reilly

     Reviewer Name: Sam Cormier
    E-mail address: sam@clark-web.com

    A must for anybody learning C++, or looking to straighten out their understanding of the language. With thorough exercises in commenting and clean code, it will help anybody to make the lives of the rest of us programmers a little bit easier. This 'chipmunk' book ranks as a classic among the likes of the 'camel' book, or the 'tiger' book. You can't go wrong with O'Reilly.
