Results of Questionnaire

This questionnaire was given to ten people under twenty. It was to determine if these youths attended mass, believed in God, and had there own faith.

 

 You will see that the results are very interesting:

 

  • Four people said they are religious people.
  • Nine people said they believed in God.
  • Five people have their own faith.
  • Three people attend mass regularly.
  • Four people attend mass now and again.
  • Three people attend mass not at all.
  • Eight people think that young people don’t go to mass because it is boring.
  • Two people think that young people don’t go to mass because its time consuming.
