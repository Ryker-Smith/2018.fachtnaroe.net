Can I alter the course of asthma?

If your asthma attack is caused by allergies, you can reduce the number of your attacks by removing allergens from your home, this means removing your cats or dogs. You can ask your doctor about specific allergic to cats or dogs.

If you smoke cigarettes and have asthma, you could be making your asthma worse or you might even develop "smokers lungs" you should go to your doctor and ask about programs to help you to quit smoking.

What kinds of medications can I take for asthma?

There are two major classes of medicines used to treat asthma. The fix is symptom – reliever medications. These are medicines that are inhaled, either as a mist or a fine powder, into your lungs and relax the muscles around the airways to make your breathing brands of reliever medication.
