Crystals

Crystals and gemstones have always been highly prized for their healing andspiritual properties. Costly and difficult to acquire, gemstones have adorned the rich and powerful for thousands of years. Not only is this a display of wialth, it also expersses the hope that the qualities and virtues thought to reside in the stones themselves would "Rub Off" on their wearers.

Healers, Shamans, priests and spiritual seekers have continually been drawn to the clarity and purity of crystals and have made use of their special powers. Crystal healing as if is developing today continues this widespread tradition by exploring new ways in which the mineral kingdom can help us restore balance to our frantic and stressful lives.

Science has yet to discover what is actually occurring during crystal healing, yet this is no way diminishes the real changes that are clearly felt by participants and the lasting benefits that can be gained.
