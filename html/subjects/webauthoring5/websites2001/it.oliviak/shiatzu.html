SHIATZU

Shiatzu is an ancient eastern European technique, which requires the therapist to apply pressure with his/her fingers, elbows, knees and feet. It works from the premise that all the energies in the body flow along ancient lines known as meridians, and these are prone to blockage. Shiatzu aims to restore the flow of energy by unblocking these lines using specific pressure points on the body. It is claimed that Shiatzu massage will rid the body of disease by restoring the natural flow of energy.
