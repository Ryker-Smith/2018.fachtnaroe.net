TERMS OF REFERENCE

 

 

The purpose of this web site is to highlight the need for immediate action to prevent further flooding to the town of Clonmel.

The web site will identify and examine various possible causes for the flooding, the effects thereof and will suggest possible solutions.

The information for the web site was obtained from the following sources:

  • Clonmel Library

  • The Internet

  • Tipperary South Riding County Council

  • Clonmel Corporation

  • Office of Public Works

  • Questionnaires

  • Local Newspapers i.e. ‘The Nationalist’ and ‘South Tipp Today’
