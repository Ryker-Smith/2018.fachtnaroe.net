RESULTS OF QUESTIONNAIRE

 

 

  • 90% of the people surveyed believe that there is a link between the flooding and the developments on the floodplains.

  • 30% believe that there could be an improvement made with regard to the emergency services.

  • 80% were dissatisfied with the Planning Authority’s developments along the quay.

  • 90% believe that global warming is a major contributor to the flooding.

  • 95% suffered financial loss.
