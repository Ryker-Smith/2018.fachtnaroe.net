JOHN SQUIRE

[Previous] [Index] [Next]

  • Stone Roses guitarist/songwriter/cover art

  • Born John Thomas Squire Broadheath, Lancanshire 24/11/62

  • Was a member of from their beginnings in 1984 as The Patrol. Has produced the cover artwork for every single official Stone Roses release with exception of "Sally Cinnamon", "Fools Gold ‘95" and "Crimson Tonight". He left the band in April 1996 and was replaced by Aziz Ibrahim.

  • Currently occupied as The Seahorses guitarist/songwriter:

"Love is the Law" single

"Do it yourself"

"Blinded by the Sun" single

"Love Me and Leave Me" single

"You Can Talk to Me" single

  • Chris Helme and Sturt Fletcher are also involved in the song writing as well as singing and playing bass respectively.

  • "You get a painting free with every record"
