MANI

[Previous] [Index] [Next]

  • Stone Roses Bassist/Songwriter

  • Born Gary Michael Mounfield, Greater Manchester 16/11/62

  • Mani replaced Pete Garner for the 1988 "Elephant Stone" sessions and stayed with the band until their 1996 Reading Festival break up. Played alongside John Squire in pre-Roses band The Mill, met Squire and Brown on their way to put up an advert for new bass player and was quickly takern into the fold.

  • Currently employed as Primal Scream bass player:

"Kowlalski" single

"Vanishing Point" album

"Star" single

"Burning Wheel" single

"If They Move Kill ‘Em" single

  • Recorded instrumental track "Morassi" (featured on Total Guitar Magazine cover CD) with Aziz Ibrahim and also played bass on Ian Brown’s solo album track "Can’t See Me"

  • "Psychedelia’s just an excuse for media men with nothing to write to make sweeping generalisations about you"
