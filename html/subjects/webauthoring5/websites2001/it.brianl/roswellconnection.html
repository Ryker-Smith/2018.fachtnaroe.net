The Roswell Connection

The main rumor goes as such, although I have heard many variations, it is said that a flying saucer crashed in the desert near Roswell, New Mexico in July of 1947. The Army secured the area and hushed-up the story. The pieces were whisked away to Texas, then to Washington D.C. where the authorities could decide on a course of action. It was decided to take the wreckage to Wright-Parterson Army Air Field, a facility advanced enough to deal with "back-engineering". This is a method used to find out how the material work and if good discoveries where made they could try use their new knowledge to advance there own technology. The story goes that the facility was too busy to keep such a sensitive secret, so another location had to be found, preferably an area that was isolated away from the public. The place they found was a very desolate lakebed in the Nevada desert, Groom Lake. According to most timelines this occured around 1952.
