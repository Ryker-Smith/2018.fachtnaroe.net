Introduction to Area 51



Area 51 since first used in the early 1950’s has been one of the most widely talked about places on the face of the earth. Although this is a widely discussed topic it has been kept as one of the worlds best kept secrets. The people involved in running it refuse to discuss what goes on in the facility. This seems to most people some what strange as there are several hundred people working there.

Most people believe that Area 51 is a secret military base where the American government are making weapons and military aircraft. It is also a widely thought issue that the American government have extra terrestrial space craft kept there. Even with all these speculations, as of yet there is still no solid prove of what goes on in the base. This is mainly due to the amount and dept of extreme security around and through out the base. To date the best pictures of the camp are from the distant mountains and that is as close as the public can go.
