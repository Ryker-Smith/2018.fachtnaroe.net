IAAF

(International Amateur Athletic Federation)

The International Amateur Athletic Federation was founded in 1912 by 17 national athletic federations who saw the need for a governing authority, for standardised world records and equipment and for an athletic programme.

Organised athletics thrived in well-developed societies and an ever-expanding competition programme developed in schools and universities and in private clubs.
