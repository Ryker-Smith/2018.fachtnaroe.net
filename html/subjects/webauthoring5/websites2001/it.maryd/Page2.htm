Gymnastics and Education:

The relationship between the world of play and the world of education was one of the main issues of the 19th Century.

Modern sports and Gymnastics:

The historical picture of this period shows gymnastics and modern sports as being institutionalised and very much isolated from every one. Modern sports were a more recent issue, coming from England, it was oriented to the elite. Gymnastics on the other hand was a much older issue, coming from Germany; it had a strong educational and military objective.
