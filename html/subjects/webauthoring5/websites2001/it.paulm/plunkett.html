The 1916 Easter Rising

Joseph Mary Plunkett (1887-1916)

was born in Dublin. He joined the Volunteers and was one of the first to be a member of the military council. He tried to assist Casement in Germany in a quest for arms in 1915 but concealed the fact that Casement had strongly advised against a rising. He was in the GPO durning Easter Week and executed on 4 May after a prision marriage to artist Grace Gifford.
