The 1916 Easter Rising

Thomas MacDonagh (1878-1916)

joined the Gaelic League in 1901 and helped Pearse to establish St Enda's in 1908. He was founding member of the Irish Volunteers in 1913 and its director of training. He joined the IRB on cooption to the military council and commanded the garrison at Jacob's factory in Bishop Street. He was executed on 3 MAy 1916, along with Pearse and CLarke
