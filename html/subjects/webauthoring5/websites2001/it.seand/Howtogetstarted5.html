HOW TO GET STARTED:

 

If you are interested in becoming involved then the first step is to contact Motorsport Ireland, the governing body of the sport, at phone Ol-6775628. Motorsport lreland issue competition licenses and we annually publish a "Yearbook" containing all the rules and regulations for Irish Motorsport. A "Yearbook" is issued free with every competition license or alternatively you my buy a copy costing £12.50. A calendar of events is also published annually and costs £1.50.

Many clubs run also Navigation Classes. These are highly recommended for newcomers to the sport as they provide invaluable assistance with pre-event and car preparation.

An up to date list of Affiliated Clubs, contact names and telephone numbers etc. is available from Motorsport Ireland on request.
