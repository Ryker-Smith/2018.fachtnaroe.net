PROFESSIONAL DECORATING

Applying the finishing coats to ceilings, walls and woodwork.

Ceilings.
With a 4" Emulsion Brush cut in around all the edges of the ceiling.  Bring the paint down about 2" onto the walls.  This will ensure that all the joins between the walls and the ceiling are covered.  It may look yntidy now but when the wall colour is applied it will cove any of the ceiling colour.  Make sure you cut in around the ceiling rose (light fitting).  Once the ceiling has been cut in using a roller apply a coat over the whole surface as shown in Fig 1.


Fig 1
Walls.
Next apply a coat of the wall colout to the walls.  First cut in all the tops of the walls, then the corners between each wall, nest tops of the skirtingf and door/window frames and finally any fittings on the walls i.e. light switches, wall sockets fitted shelving.  As with the ceilin when all the walls have been cut in using a roller apply a coat of paint to the walls as shown in Fig 2.
