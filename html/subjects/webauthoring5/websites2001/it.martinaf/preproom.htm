PROFESSIONAL DECORATING
Before decorating any room clear out everything posssible.  Remove as much furniture as possible.  Anything that cannot be removed should be place in the center of the room.  If possible take up carpets, if this is not an option or you need to protect your floor cover well dust sheets. DO NOT use polythene or plastic dust sheets to cover the floor as these are very dangerous when on ladders or even when walking on.  It is very easy to slip and when using a ladder is extremely dangerous.  Cover any furniture that must remain in the room.  Remove curtain rails if possible, lamp shades, picture hooks and door furniture.
