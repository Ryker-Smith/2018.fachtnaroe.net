PROFESSIONAL DECORATING
Types of Paint.

Water based paints:
Matt Emulsion paint is suitable for walls and ceilings.  It is a very easy paint ot work with.  Can be applied either by brush or roller.  All tools used are cleaned by using water.  Matt emulsion comes in many differnt colours and you can even have your own personal colour mixed.  This emulsion dries to a flat finish.

Vinyl Silk Emulsion has the same properties as matt emulsion, it is water based and all tools are cleand using water but it is not as easy to use.  Vinyl Silk dries with a slight sheen and is more hard wearing than matt.  The difference between the two paints is tht Vinyl Silk has Acrylic add to it.  It dries to a washable finish unlike matt.

Oil based paints:
Primer is used on bare wood that is being prepared for a pint finish.  It seals the wood and makes it less porous as well as giving the wood a key.

Undercoat is mainly used on woodwork.  It is applied after any primer hads been applied.  Even when painting over already painter woodwork undercoat should always be applied before the finishing coat.  It will cover dark colours and provide a key for the finishing paint.  Cleean all tools with white spirit.

Gloss Paint is used as a finish mainly for woodwork and metalwork.  It is a hardwearing paint and when it is dry it has a high sheen hence the name gloss.  Gloss paint needs to be applied sparingly as applying a heavy coat will end up with unsightly drips and runs.  It is better to apply 2 light coats of gloss rather thatn one heavy coat.

Eggshell Paint is suitable for walls, ceilings, and woodwork.  It is very good in areas where therte will be a lot of moisture like a kitchen or bathroom.  It is a hardwearing paint.  Eggshell is very good for walls and ceilings that are badly stained. It comes in a variety of colours.
