 Introduction

To be successful in your decorating you must plan some sort of system.  As with any project if it is well planned out your life will be made much more easy.

This website will concentrate on the preparation of the ceilings and walls of a room using paint.  There are many aspects in decorating a room like the color scheme, paint or wallpaper for the walls, curtains, pictures, flooring and lighting.  For this project I will explain in detail how to prepare walls, ceilings.
Even before you decide on the colors  the preparation of all surfaces must be done properly.  It does not matter how much money you spend on paint, if the preparation is not done properly the finish will not look good.

It is important to have the right tools for the right job.  The main tools used in the preparation of a room are scrapers, filling knives, sandpaper, steam strippers, and blow torches.  I will give a description of all these tools and the correct way in which to use them.

There is a wrong and a right way to paint walls and ceilings.  Anyone can paint a wall or ceiling but not everyone can do a good job of it.

I will also explain how to tackle most of the problems that people come across when decorating.  There is a wide variety of things that can go wrong and I will give advice on the best solution to the most common problems.

So to sum up I will demonstrate how to prepare the walls and ceilings of a room.  I will explain what equipment is required and how to use the equipment for the job.  The most important part of decorating is the preparation of the surfaces.  The secret to a good finish is good preparation.  Most of the hard work and most of the time goes into the preparation of the surfaces.  Another problem concerning decorating is that many people think that by just painting the doors or by papering or painting the walls of a room will make the room look good.  But in fact this will only show up what has not been done.  So if you want your room to look good decorate everything at the same time.
