What an individual can do is briefly stated below -:

  1. Use recycling facilities for paper, glass,

    2. plastic, cans and oils.

  2. Conserve energy at home and at work.
  3. Buy unleaded petrol.
  4. Use public transport whenever possible.
  5. Plant a tree.
  6. Stop littering and stop others also.
  7. Use smokeless and low smoke fuels only.
  8. Look for and buy environmentally friendly

    9. Products.

  9. Initiate or assist local clean up campaigns

10.Report illegal dumping and suspected air

or water pollution incidents to your local

authorities.

11.Bring your own bags to shop instead of

getting new ones each time.

So to conclude, efforts are needed both at the individual and industrial level to protect and enhance our environment for our future generations to come.
