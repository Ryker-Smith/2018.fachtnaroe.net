At present Ireland’s record rate of economic development is causing an acceleration of pressures on the environment. This follows a period of several decades during which population growth, urbanization and industrial intensification have given rise to gradually increasing environmental pressures. Over the course of the 1990’s, along with the surge in the economy, a number of strong trends were notable. These trends are briefly discussed.The economic boom in Ireland and the resulting pressures on Environment are outlined in detail in a report published by the Irish Times on 27th April 2000.
