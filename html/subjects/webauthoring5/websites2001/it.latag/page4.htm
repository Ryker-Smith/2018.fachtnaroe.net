Personal consumption of goods and services has increased by one-third in five-year period. In Ireland personal consumption of goods and services increased by over 32% from 1993 to 1998. The Personal consumption growth rate and its trends are available at the CSO Home Page.
INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTION

The volume of industrial production has more than doubled. Industry has the potential to create significant pressures on the environment through the use of dangerous substances, emission of air and water, the consumption of natural resources and energy, and the contamination of land.
