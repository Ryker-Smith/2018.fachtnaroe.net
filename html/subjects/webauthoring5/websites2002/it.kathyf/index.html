Cork International Airport is one of the fastest growing regional airports in Europe. Passenger numbers are fast approaching two million per annum.

Cork International Airport, the Gateway to the south of Ireland is easily accesible by air from Europe, the United States and Great Britain.

Cork International Airport is 8KM from Cork City. Waterford, Tipperary and Kilkenny are within easy reach via the new Lee Tunnel. A frequent Bus service operates from the Airport to the city centre. Train services operate from Kent Station serving all of Ireland.

Cork International Airport is the gateway to Ireland's finest scenery of West Cork and Killarney. There are over 100 golf courses close at hand including the world renowned Head of Kinsale, Fota, Mount Juliet, Waterville and Ballybunion. Other leisure pursuits in Cork, Kerry and Waterford include deep sea angling, sailing and equestrian sports. The gourmet capital of Ireland Kinsale will always provide an unforgettable dining experience.

The University city of Cork offers unique heritage centres such as Blarney Castle and the City Gaol. Only one hours journey takes one to the Rock of Cashel in Tipperary, the world famous Waterford Crystal factory and the wonderful Lismore Castle estate.
