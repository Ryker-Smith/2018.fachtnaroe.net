Location:

Stadium is on southside of Cork city and is the home of the Munster FA. City had a brief dalliance with their own ground in Bishopstown but the experiment, while worthy of support, was dogged by problems from the start. Poor drainage and financial difficulties proved to be insurmontable problems and the FAI stepped in to buy the facility a few years ago. Turners Cross has had significant development work, including the replacement of the main stand, done over the last few years.
