Colours

  • Browsers are dependant on the system upon which they run for their performance

  • Colours vary from system to system

  • Use of limited ‘web-safe’ colours is recommended

  • Colours are expressed in RGB format in Hex

    • FF0000 is red
    • 00FF00 is green
    • 0000FF is blue

  • Combining values gives different colours
