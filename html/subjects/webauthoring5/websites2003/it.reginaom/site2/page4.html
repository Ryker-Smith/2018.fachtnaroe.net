Warning Signs

Warning signs A, B, C, D, & E.

A for Asymmetry, as in irregular shape or one half different to the other.

B for irregular Border,

C for Colour variation,

D for Diameter greater than 6mm (size of a pencil head eraser),

E for Elevated, or raised mole

Skin cancers are most likely to appear on the face, neck, or other exposed skin. They can appear as a small lump, a flat, red spot or a firm red lump.
