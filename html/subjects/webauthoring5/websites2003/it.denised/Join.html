Join the Irish Red Cross

The Irish Red Cross Society relies on individuals to join their local branches, establish branches where none exist and donate funds. Voluntary service through membership is one of the fundamental principles of the Red Cross worldwide and is open to all.

So what do you get from the Red Cross?

Membership of a global network with 105 million members.

Access to safety and caring courses which you can use in your community.

Training in life-skills, dealing with people, administration etc.

New friends and associates in your community and throughout Ireland.

Knowledge of events throughout Ireland and around the world.

So what does the Red Cross get from you?

Access to more people who need our help at home and abroad.

Greater strength through increased numbers.

Involvement in more communities.

New skills and experience.

Increased funds.

Have you any interest in any of the following activities and/or services?

First Aid cover at events First Aid training Mountain Rescue School Safety Babysitting Courses Annual Respite Break Therapeutic Handcare Carers Courses Safe Lifting Overseas Service Fundraising Promotion
