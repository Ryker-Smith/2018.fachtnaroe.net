Visitor Guidelines

Take Care:
Much of the Park is accessible to the visitor. However, the animals do not like to be harassed and we would encourage you to
stay on the paths as much as possible. These paths allow you to see most of the Park without difficulty.

Watching Animals:
Most animals allow a close approach. Your visit will be most enjoyable if you do not try to pet them as this would only frighten them.
If you stand back a short distance you will see the animals behaving naturally and calmly.
Of course, chasing the animals is forbidden.

Feeding:
The animals are fed special diets, in most cases twice a day. We particularly ask you not to feed the animals as this can damage
their health or even cause their death.
