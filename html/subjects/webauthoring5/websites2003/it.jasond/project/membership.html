There are two choices of great value-for-money memberships at FOTA Wildlife Park,
Conservation Membership and Park Membership:

Conservation Membership allows free entry to:

  • 4 adults (including the member) or
  • 3 adults (including the member) + 2 children* or
  • 2 adults (including the member) + 4 children* or
  • 1 adult member + 6 children*

Park Membership allows free entry to:

  • 2 adults (including the member)
  • 1 adult member + 2 children*

Note:
- toddlers up to 2 years of age enter free
*children are classified as 3-16 years of age
