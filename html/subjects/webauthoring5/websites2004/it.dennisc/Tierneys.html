Sean Tierneys Bar Clonmel

Is situated in O'Connell St. Clonmel, a town with many Historical associations and picturesquely located beneath Slievenamon and the close Comeragh Mountains.

It won many awards through the years for it's excellent cuisine, Munster Pub of the Year Award, Club Orange & Licensing World Pub Lunch Award, Cork Dry Gin Pub Enterprise Award, and Irish Pub of Distinction Award.

Food is served daily from 10 am until Late. The bar is decorated in both modern and traditional styles. The beauty of Clonmel is reflected by the pictures that adorn the walls. Complementing this charm is a myriad of well chosen artifacts of bygone days.

Upstairs is the renowned Comeragh Restaurant with two dining rooms, the Long Hill Room and the Slievenamon Room, idealy suited to private lunches and dinner parties as well as business functions. Over the years it has gained a reputation for quality food, rounding of the menu with a selection of quality wines.
