Fennessys Hotel

Old established Hotel.

A beautiful Georgian building, it was fully restored and refurbished recently. It was originally the home of John Hackett, Mayor of Clonmel in the 19th Centuary. Family run hotel. Elegant decor throughout.

Located in the town center, Gladstone St. Clonmel opposite the church.

Shops, pubs and church all at the doorstep.

Evening entertainment in the bar.
