Allens Bar & Restaurant

John Allen's Bar is situated in Lower Irishtown between the beautiful Parish Church of St. Mary's and the Historic West Gate.

It's setting is particularly lovely with a garden to the rear of the premises leading down to the River Suir, and sits in the shade of the Comeragh Mountains.

It is family owned and managed. Serving good wholesome food daily it's a must whilst traveling through the sunny South East. A favorite with all our guests is Tipperary organic ice-cream, so go ahead and treat yourself.
