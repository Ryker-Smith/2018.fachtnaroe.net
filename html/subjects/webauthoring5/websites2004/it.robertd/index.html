Clonmel Co.Tipperary

Clonmel, A medieval town, is situated on the river Suir, with the Comeragh Mountains to the South and the mountain of Slievenamon to the East. The river Suir has been a major influence on the town's development, a fact reflected in the town's crest showing a bridge across a river with three fish under underneath.
