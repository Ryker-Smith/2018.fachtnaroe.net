South Tipperary Alcohol & Addiction Service, Coolgreany House, Queen St, Clonmel, Co. Tipperary.

St. Michael's Unit, St. Joseph's Hospital, Clonmel, Co. Tipperary.

Alcoholics Anonymous, there are AA meetings in all the major towns in South Tipperary.

What These Groups Do:

1 St. Michael's Unit offers the alcoholic a drying out period in a cloistered environment as well as counselling.
2The South Tipperary Alcohol & Addiction Service offers addiction counselling, assessment and detox treatment along with family counselling.
3AA offers a twelve-step program to help alcoholics help themselves by applying the program to their lives while staying dry.

What is there to help the Affected Families?

1: Al-Anon

This is a 12 step program dedicated to helping the husbands/wives and the adult children if alcoholics. The only requirement for membership is that there be a problem of alcoholism in a family member.

2: Alateen

This is a 12-step program dedicated to helping the adolescent children of alcoholics. The only requirement for membership is that there be a problem of alcoholism in a family member.
