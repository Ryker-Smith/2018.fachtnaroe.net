Welcome to Sport In Clonmel.

Our website is an introduction to the many sporting activities which are present in Clonmel.

Clonmel has a lot to offer the sports fanatics, golf, tennis, horse racing etc. Aswell as the traditional irish sports, hurling and football. Clonmel has a proud tradition of GAA sucess with players such as Declan Browne, a two time All-Star being the most famous.
