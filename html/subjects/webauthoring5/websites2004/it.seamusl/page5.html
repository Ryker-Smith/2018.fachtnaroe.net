Frank Patterson

Although Frank Patterson Patterson died many feel before his time he led a full and active life. Amoung his achievments where singing for the president of the America and the of course the Pope.

While Frank's life on this earth ended on June 10, 2000, he lives on through his music and through the many lives he touched with his gentle and generous ways."
