Why people Smoke?

 Most teenagers take up smoking as an experiment. This experimentation usually becomes an addiction. Even though they are aware of the dangers of smoking, they don’t think it can happen to them.

Others are lead it into it by peer pressure and by their friends influences. It is their way to rebel against the older generation.

If even one parent smokes it is very likely that the child will also smoke. Children look up to their parents for guidance and when they see a parent smoking they will think its all right and couldn’t be dangerous.

The smoking rate with teenage girls is a lot higher than the smoking rate with teenage boys. This maybe because a lot of young girls worry a lot about their weight so they think by smoking it will keep their weight down.

One of the biggest influences on the younger generation is advertising. Most adds are aimed at the young because they replace the older generation of smokers when they die.

There are no serious laws against underage smokers. Cigarettes are easily consumed from a vending machine.
