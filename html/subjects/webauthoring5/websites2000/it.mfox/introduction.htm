PROFESSIONAL DECORATING
Introduction

This website will concentrate on the preparaion of the ceiling and walls of a room that are to be finished with paint.  To be successful in your decorating you must prepare the surface properly first.  Although there are many aspects in decorating a room such as color scheme, paint or wallpaper, curtains, pictures, flooring and lighting.  For this project I will explain in detail how to prepare walls and the ceiling.  Even before you decide on colors or wallcoverings the preparation of all surfaces must be done properly.  It does not matter how much money you spend on wallpaper or special paints,  if the preparation is not done properly the finish will not look good.

It is important to have the right tools for the right job.  The main tools used in the preparation of a room are scrapers, filling knives, sandpaper, steam strippers, and blow torches.  I will give a description of all these tools and the correct way in which to use them.

Once all the preparation has been done I will explain how to put the finishing coats to all surfaces.  There is a wrong and a right way to paint walls and ceilings.  Anyone can paint a wall or ceiling but not everyone can do a good job of it.

As in the preparation it is also important to have the right tools for the finishing off.  The main tools for this part of the job are brushes and rollers.  Brushes come in a variety of sizes and shapes which I will go into detail about.

When the walls and ceilings have been finished next the woodwork must be finished.  Woodwork and metalwork also have a wrong and a right way of being painted.

I will also explain how to tackle most of the problems that people come across when decorating.  There are a wide variety of things that can go wrong and I will give advice on the best solution to the most common problems.

So to sum up I will demonstrate how to prepare and decorate the ceiling and walls of  a room from start to finish.  I will explain what equipment is required and how to use the equipment for the job.  The most important part of decorating is the preparation of the surfaces.  The secret to a good finish is good preparation.  Most of the hard work and most of the time goes into the preparation of the surfaces.
