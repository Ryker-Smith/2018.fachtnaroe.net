FORMATION OF TOTTENHAM

TOTTENHAM HOTSPUR Was founded in 1882 they were first a cricket team that wanted to do something while the cricket season was over. Tottenham Hotspur Is one of the most famous names in English football, They have won the League Championship and F.A. Cup a eight times Man Utd have Held a record of 10 times; the League Championship twice; and three major European trophies along the way, which were the Cup winners cup And two the UEFA cup.
The greatest triumph our history was undoubtedly the League Championship and F.A.Cup double of
1960-6, We were the first team to win the "magical" double this century---a feat which put spurs at the pinnacle of the game in England.

After the formation of Tottenham they Slowly began to improve their game and began to play tougher fixtures; but it was not until 1892-93 that we first began playing in a league. This was an amateur league as the South of England opposed the adoption of professional status. This is one reason why the northern teams have dominated professional football in England.
