Nevada U.S Military

Two methods of execution.

Prisoners have choice between electric chair and lethal injection.

Lethal gas will be used if lethal injection is ever held unconstitutional.

If lethal injection is ever held unconstitutional than electric chair will be used, and if both are ever held unconstitutional than the firing squad will be used.

Authorises lethal injection for offenders convicted on or after 25/3/94, those convicted before this date may select lethal injection or lethal gas.

Tennessee authorises lethal injection for those sentenced after Jan 1,1999 and those currently on death row will choose between the electric chair and lethal injection.

New Hampshire authorises hanging only if lethal injection cannot be given.
