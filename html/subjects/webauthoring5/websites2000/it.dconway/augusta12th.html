12th hole – Golden Bell
(Championship 155yd Par3)

This is the second of three difficult holes that make up the famous Amen Corner –
which has dashed many a Masters player's hopes. It's a tricky hole to play as you
cannot feel the strength of the wind when you weigh up your shot on the tee. There
are two common mistakes to avoid. The first is not selecting the right club, so the
ball fails short into the creek. Another problem occurs if the ball lands on the bank
behind the green. From there your best bet is to leave the chip or bunker shot short
- it's all too easy to run past the hole into the water.
