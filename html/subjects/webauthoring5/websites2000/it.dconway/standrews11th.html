11th hole – High
(Championship and medal 172yd Par 3)
 

The green is on the edge of the River Eden and exposed to the wind, making club
selection a problem. Anything short is likely run into large, deep Hill bunker on
the left, or the small, deep Strath bunker on the right. If you land in either, consider
playing out backwards. Make sure you don’t go over the back – you’re in thick
rough playing to a downslope. The putting surface is always very quick with a great
deal of borrow.
