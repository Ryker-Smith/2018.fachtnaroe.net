15th hole- Fire Thorn
(Championship 500yd Par 5)

Your tee shot can go a long way off line without getting into trouble, but you're
better off trying for a shot from the right of the fairway to find the green. Don't
go for the green in 2 - lay up short of the pond and chip in for 3. If the wind is
behind you, it's difficult to hold the green, especially when the flag is on the left
where the green is shallow. It's risky to putt from the back of the green ~ the
ball could run down the slope into the pond.
