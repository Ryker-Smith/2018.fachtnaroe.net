Some relevant DTP factoids:

  • An A4 page is 210mm x 297mm in size.
  • An A3 page is twice the area of an A4 sheet, while an A5 page is half the area.
  • A compliment slip is 210mm x 90mm in order to fit comfortably inside a DL envelope.
  • A DL envelope (whether windowed or not) is 220mm x 110mm.
  • A normal business card is 90mm x 50mm but these dimensions may vary to make the card easier to store in a wallet or a card holder.
  • Normal copier & printer paper is 80gsm in weight.
  • Most printers have an upper limit on the gsm weight of paper they can handle. Typically this is about 100-110gsm. Above this weight you are beginning to deal with card.
