Wet-Ink: Inkjet Printer

  • Inexpensive

  • Cheap access to colour

  • Quiet

  • Very good graphics

  • Relatively slow

  • Not suitable for multi-part stationery

  • Uses fine droplets of ink

  • Print quality measured in DPI (dots per inch)

    • 720dpi is generally satisfactory for photo-quality printing
