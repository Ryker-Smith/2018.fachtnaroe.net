ISDN

  • Integrated Services Digital Network

  • Higher bandwidth than POTS

  • Will replace standard Packet Switched Telephone Network (PSTN)

  • Can support video-conferencing etc

  • Becoming more affordable and available in Ireland

  • Terminal Adapters (TA) and Network Terminators (NT) used instead of modem and standard phone socket
