Hard Disk Capacities

  • Supported IDE hard drive size is determined by a number of factors, including the construction of the drive itself, the operating system, and the system BIOS.

  • As of April 1998, there were four categories of IDE hard drive size support among system BIOSes motherboards: 512 MB (528,482,304 bytes), 2.1 GB (2,147,483,648 bytes), 8.4 GB (8,455,200,768 bytes), and greater than 8.4 GB (the theoretical maximum of this category is 137,438,953,472 bytes or 137.4 GB).
