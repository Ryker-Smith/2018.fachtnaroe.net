Processing Speed Is Measured In MHz

  • Hz = Hertz

  • 1Hz = 1 Cycle per second

  • MHz = 1,000,000 cycles per second

  • Original PC operated at 4.77MHz

  • Todays Pentiums operate at up to 1,000MHz

  • Clock multiplying is used to achieve high speeds at affordable costs

  • Clock speed is not a reliable indicator of throughput
