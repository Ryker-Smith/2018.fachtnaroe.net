DVD

  • Digital Video Disk, Digital Versatile Disk, - now just D-V-D

  • Each side holds 4.7 GB, equal to 7 CD-ROM's of data

  • Maximum dual-layer capacity is 19GB, equal to 28 CD-ROM’s of data

  • Projected to replace CD disks and VHS tapes

  • Disks currently need to be turned to use both sides
