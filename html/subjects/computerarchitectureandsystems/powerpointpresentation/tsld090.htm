Serial and Parallel Communications

  • Most PCs have two standard serial ports, called COM1 and COM2

    • Ports are called ttyS0 and ttyS1 under UNIX

  • Most PCs have one parallel port, called LPT1

    • Up to two more can be added

  • Additional ports may be added, but resource conflicts may arise

  • Parallel transmission is faster
