CD-ROM

  • 650 MB capacity - equivalent to about 250,000 pages of text or 20,000 medium-resolution images

  • Disks are inexpensive to produce

  • May be on SCSI or IDE bus

  • Transfer rates now – theoretically - very fast

  • Supported natively by newer operating systems

    • DOS needs special support
