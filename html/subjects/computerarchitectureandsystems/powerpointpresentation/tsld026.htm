Memory Capacity

  • The smallest unit of storage is the bit, consisting of a 1 or a 0

  • The smallest used storage amount is the byte - eight bits allowing for 256 unique combinations of 1 and 0

  • The kilobyte (KB) is 1024 bytes

  • The megabyte (MB) is 1024 kilobytes, or 1,048,576 bytes
