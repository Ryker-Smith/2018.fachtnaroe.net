Serial Transmission Rates

  • Transmission measured in Bits Per Second (bps) or baud.

  • 16550 UART may function up to 115,000 bps (115Kbps)

  • Modems operate up to 33.6Kbps

  • 56K modems don’t give actual 56K speeds

  • ISDN Terminal adapters function at 64Kbps

    • Channels may be bundled

  • Compression speeds up actual throughput
