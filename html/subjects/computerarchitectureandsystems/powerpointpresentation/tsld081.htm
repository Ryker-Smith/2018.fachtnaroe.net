PC (Image) Scanners

  • May be flatbed, pass-over, or pull-through

  • Quality of scan measured in Dots Per Inch (DPI)

  • Typical scans from 300 - 2400 DPI

  • Output DPI is critical

  • Can input text in conjunction with Optical Character Recognition software (OCR)

  • Normally used for inputting graphics

  • Are now highly affordable (IEP80 minimum)
