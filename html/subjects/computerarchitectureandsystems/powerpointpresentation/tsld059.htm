Tape Drive Performance

  • Large amounts of storage available at low cost

  • Compression often supported

  • SCSI tape drives operate at SCSI rates

  • Sequential access means initial seek delays

  • Improved materials mean better performance and reliability

  • Network backups normally use DAT tapes, though QIC and TRAVAN technologies were once common

  • Overnight backups compensate for slow drive speeds
