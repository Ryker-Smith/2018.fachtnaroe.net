Mouse

  • Modern PC mouse has two or more buttons

  • Right mouse unused before Windows 95

  • Apple mouse has one button

  • Most difficult computer peripheral for most novice users

  • May connect via PS/2, standard serial or USB port

  • Cheap, from £8-100

  • Need periodic cleaning

  • Often opto-mechanical

    • Non-mechanical now available
