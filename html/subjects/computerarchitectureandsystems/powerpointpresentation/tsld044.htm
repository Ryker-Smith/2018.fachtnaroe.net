Floppy Disks

  • Capacities of 1.2, 1.44 and 2.88 MB

  • Becoming obsolete, developed in 1970’s

  • Drives are inexpensive, c£15-20

  • Disks are inexpensive, c£0.80 each

  • Used for moving data, installation programs, small backups

  • Relatively slow operation due to initial spin-up delay
