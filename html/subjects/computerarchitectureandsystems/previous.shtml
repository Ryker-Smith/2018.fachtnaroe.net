  • Exam 2014 (40%) with suggested solutions.
  • Research Assignment: 60%
  • Combined Studies Project: - Home office for a web-business.
    Strange though it may sound, this isn't actually a hard assignment! Most people already have a system at home which, with a bit more work, could be used as a business system. Think about it; what do you really need? Computers can be bought in ollmhargadhs and professional document production software is given away free. So what else do you need? Make a list. Choose functions. Find suppliers. Compare prices. Put it all together with your recommendations. This assignment is less about technology and more about making decisions about technology.

Assessment 2012/2013:

  • Exam (40%): the paper is here with suggested solutions..
  • Research Assignment: 60%
  • Combined Studies Project: - Quinlough Hostel & Adventure Centre - business network with in-house web reservations system. Simplification follows:

    Price follows function, function follows requirements.

    First research your requirements (what the system should do for its users).

    Then break this down as a list of functions (e.g. if the requirement is the recording of bookings, what are the tools/items that provide the functions/actions that will make this possible).

    Next find prices for the items/tools that provide those functions.

    Carry out research, use a methodology planner, use focus sheets and make lists!
