The Accounts Officer in St. Paul’s wishes to send invoices only to those patients who have either been admitted to one of the Hospital’s wards or sent for X ray. You are asked to produce these mailing labels. For logistical reasons the Accounts Officer requests that the labels be printed in a particular sequence:-
1. Ascending order on the consultant field (primary sort field)
2. Descending order on the charge field (secondary sort field)
Each label should show the date as well as the patient’s name, address and record number. The actual printing of the labels can take place at the end of the examination if you so wish.
