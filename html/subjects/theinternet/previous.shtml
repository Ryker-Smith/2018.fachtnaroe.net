    (This assignment is also to be submitted for Communications, for which it is worth 25% of the marks.)
    I won't say it bothers me, but ... When you think of people like Denis Ritche, Tommy Flowers, Alan Turing - there are so many super sharp people who get overlooked. But though those ppl all were brilliant for their time, they're not brilliant for this one. There are just so many brilliant people, we don't know what to do with them, so we wreck their heads before it's too late. Self-defense. Still, at least we clever few know of these true greats and not just the more famous ones that the less informed can only think of. And wish they were wealthy as. Not as sharp as. Just wealthy. As if they would be as happy with the wealth, without the wisdom in the first place.
