History of Internet

Brief

The Internet is a huge computer network linking together millions of smaller networks all over the world. A massive amount of information is stored on these computers. When you connect to the Internet you have a have access to sites all over the world.

People are using the Internet these days like never before. People use the Internet to surf for information, chat rooms, and E-mail which is used to communicate with family and friends all over the world.

The Internet is also used for on-line bookings, ticket sales, shopping, investments and banking on-line etc.

Since the Internet is all over the world no country has complete owner ship over it. The Internet originated from America. Scientists invented the Internet. As long as the scientists and engineers run the Internet and not the countries and the companies the standards been kept open the Internet will continue to grow smoothly.

Networking

The first description of the social interaction that could be enabled through networking were memos in August 1962 by a fellow called J.C.R Licklider. He described his Galactic Network and envisioned a globally interconnect set of computers through which everyone could access data and programs from any site the user entered. Networking really started from this point in October 1962.
