Internet E-mail

Electronic E-mail or "e-mail" is the most common form of communication on the internet. You start by creating a message in an electronic mail form. Then, choose the recipient’s e-mail address and send it with a click on a button. The mail message gets sent to the receiving computer-whether it’s in Tokyo, Japan, downtown Chicago or your neighbour’s house-sometimes within seconds.

Unique addresses this is how they work. The first part of the address identifies the individual recipient. The second part includes the "domain name," The third part includes "com" tells you the company is a business, or commercial organisation.
